Welcome to Sharing Commons

You've accessed a preview of Sharing Commons, a web app created to make sharing easier. The app is under active developement, changing almost daily. If you've been invited to give the app a try, visit the join page to get started. If you come across any bugs, or have suggestions to improve the usability or functionality of Sharing Commons, please share your comments through the Report a Bug page (the link is found in the footer of every page). Please be patient with the Sharing Commons team as we build the product. Thanks!

What is Sharing Commons?

Sharing Commons is a sharing network, built to help users share books between friends. With Sharing Commons you can create an index, or library, of all your personal books, browse your friends' libraries, and request to loan items from your friends. Sharing Commons tracks the items you are loaning and borrowing, and notifies you when items are due. Join today!
